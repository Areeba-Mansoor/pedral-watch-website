Welcome to Pedral Watches

At Pedral Watches, we believe that timekeeping is more than just a function—it's an art form. The journey began with a dream and a defiant spirit determined to forge a unique path in the world of horology. Our story is not one of tradition but of innovation and unwavering resilience. From the heart of Stockholm, we create timepieces that blend the boldness of retro-futurism with the minimalist elegance of Swedish design. Each watch is a testament to our belief that true designs barriers are the ones we set for ourselves.

A rare collection within your reach

Each model in our collection radiates an audacious retro-futurism, balanced by minimalist elegance— a nod to the rich essence of understated Swedish design.The vibrant colour palettes of the dial echoes the diversity of cultures and places across the globe, shaping timepieces that are as captivating as they are reliable.Our refusal to compromise allows us to explore uncharted artistic territory and craft timepieces with fluid, organic lines that seamlessly embrace your wrist. To ensure exceptional service and cultivate a special bond with every collector, we limit annual production to a mere 20 pieces per model release and color.
